Welcome to **Cakeberries-by_Thas**! This repository is dedicated to our passion for creating delightful and delicious cakes. Our project aims to showcase a variety of cake designs, recipes, and baking tips for all cake enthusiasts.

## Project Overview
CakeberriesDelights is a static webpage designed to inspire and guide both amateur and experienced bakers in their cake-making journey. Here, you'll find:
- **Elegant Cake Designs:** From classic to contemporary, browse through a collection of beautifully crafted cakes.
- **Tried-and-True Recipes:** Follow our detailed recipes to bake cakes that are as tasty as they are stunning.
- **Baking Tips and Tricks:** Learn from our experiences with tips to perfect your baking skills.

## Features
- **Responsive Design:** Our webpage is optimized for all devices, ensuring a seamless browsing experience.
- **Interactive Gallery:** Explore our cake gallery with high-quality images and descriptions.
- **Recipe Cards:** Easy-to-follow recipes for each cake, including ingredients, steps, and baking times.
- **Contact Form:** Reach out to us for baking advice, collaborations, or custom cake orders.
